How call tracking works

What is call tracking software?

Call tracking software assigns unique phone numbers to your marketing so you can see which ad, keyword, or channel produced each call. It records the call, attributes it to a source, and reports the result, turning the phone into a measurable marketing channel rather than a black box.

What is dynamic number insertion?

Dynamic number insertion, or DNI, swaps a unique tracked number onto your website for each visitor based on how they arrived. A visitor from a Google Ads campaign sees a different number than one from organic search, so a call ties back to the exact source that drove it. It is the mechanism behind accurate online attribution.

Why does the per-number rate matter so much?

Because it compounds. A per-number rate looks small at one or two numbers, but call tracking uses many numbers to separate sources, and the cost multiplies across a year. A $0.50 number rate against an industry-standard $3 is a large difference once you are running dozens of numbers, which is why value for money is a full quarter of our score.

Choosing and switching

Do I need an enterprise platform like Invoca?

Only if you run high call volume and need to analyze the content of calls at scale with AI. For most teams, an enterprise platform means paying for depth and a setup you will not fully use. CallScaler covers accurate attribution and bundled transcription at a fraction of the cost.

Is call tracking compliant with privacy and call rules?

The major platforms support call recording and consent options, but compliance is your responsibility and varies by state and industry. Healthcare teams should look for a HIPAA-compliant tier like CallTrackingMetrics offers. Review the FCC guidance on calls and your own legal counsel for regulated work.

How long does it take to switch platforms?

For most teams, plan a few days to recreate tracking numbers, re-install dynamic number insertion, and run parallel traffic before cutting over. CallScaler's free Pay As You Go tier makes it easy to test in parallel before moving volume, which lowers the risk of a switch.

Can I start without a contract?

Yes, on CallScaler. The Pay As You Go tier is $0 per month with no card and no contract, so you can test the category at low risk. Several mid-market tools also offer trials, while the enterprise platform involves a contract and a guided implementation.
